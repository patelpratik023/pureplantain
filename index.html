More Than Just Flour.
It's Functional Nutrition.

In a world obsessed with "Gluten-Free" and "Gut Health," Green Banana Flour is the industry's best-kept secret. Sourced from the nutrient-dense soils of Nandurbar, Maharashtra, our raw green bananas are harvested at the peak of their green stage—before the sugar develops.

The result? A neutral-tasting powder that is nearly **50% Resistant Starch** by weight. It acts not just as a flour, but as a powerful prebiotic supplement that thickens sauces, binds doughs, and boosts the nutritional profile of any product without altering its flavor.

  • Clean Label: Single ingredient, no gums or additives.
  • Superior Binding: Replaces gums in gluten-free baking.
  • Cost Effective: Use 30% less volume than wheat flour.
